the complete experience

Welcome to The Pointe Restaurant, an aspirational fine dining experience on the cusp of Canada’s wild west coast. With a sweeping 240-degree view of the lively Pacific Ocean, innovative Canadian cuisine and refined service, at The Pointe we invite you to a taste of all the best things our coast has to offer. Whether you’re celebrating a special weekend or a relaxed gathering with friends, our personable attention to detail will make your visit unique and memorable.

LET US NOURISH YOU

Our sophisticated menus feature inventive contemporary dishes inspired by fresh, local and seasonal fare. With a unique approach to gastronomy complemented by an extensive wine selection and a spectacular vista, The Pointe indulges all of the senses.

RAISE A GLASS

Featuring both Canadian winemakers and international vineyards, Howard's Wine Cellar is an unparalleled opportunity for wine lovers and wine scholars alike. This exceptional venue is available for intimate dining experiences and small groups by special arrangement.

RELAX AND UNWIND

Enjoy a classic cocktail, a cheeky pint or flight of scotches at our On the Rocks Bar. Here we feature an excellent selection of Pacific Northwest wines, a quality range of classic ports and one of B.C.'s largest selection of single-malt scotches.

We let the seasons dictate how we write our menus. We change them four times a year, and we have a new tasting menu weekly, which allows us to utilize those fantastic products that come and go within weeks.

Chef Warren Barr
1 june 2018
